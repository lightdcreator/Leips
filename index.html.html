Trade-In Concerns:

  • Are you worried about being charged for the trade-in after processing?
  • Could you share details about the device you traded in?

Refund Timeline: If you returned an item in-store within the 15-day window, refunds are processed within 10 business days. Once approved, the funds will be credited to your original payment method. Keep in mind that your bank may take an additional 5–7 business days for processing.

    CFQA Notes (Key Questions & Answers)

    Why Can’t I Place an Order?

    • Clear your browser's cache and cookies.
    • Try ordering from a different device or payment method.
    • Use the Samsung Shop App or website interchangeably.

    Why is My Order Late? We’re working to ship orders as quickly as possible. You’ll receive a shipment notification email once it’s ready.

    Whys is my order missing items https://order-help.us.samsung.com/hc/en-us/articles/360058953673-Why-is-my-order-missing-items

    How Do I Track My Trade-In Status? You’ll receive an email when your trade-in is processed. You can also check the status via "My Orders."

    https://order-help.us.samsung.com/hc/en-us/articles/360055936554-What-is-Samsung-s-trade-in-program-and-what-are-the-requirements

    Rejected Trade-In Devices: If your trade-in is rejected, you’ll be notified by email. You have 7 days to request a return of the device. If no request is made, the trade-in discount applied at checkout will be charged to your payment method.

    Refund for Samsung Rewards Points: Yes, points redeemed on returned orders are refunded within 5 days of return processing.
